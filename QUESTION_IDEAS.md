# Additional Question Ideas

These ideas were checked against the current question bank in `src/data.js`.
Existing categories already cover flags, capitals, borders, broad location
regions, physical geography, landmarks, wildlife, history/former names, and one
shape prompt. The categories below are intended to add new kinds of knowledge
rather than more of those same buckets. For current-stat or membership-based
ideas, verify the source data before converting them into playable questions.

## Candidate Categories

### Language

Ask for countries by official or widely used language.

- Portuguese-speaking countries: Portugal, Brazil, Angola, Mozambique, Cabo Verde, Guinea-Bissau, Sao Tome and Principe, Timor-Leste, Equatorial Guinea.
- Arabic-speaking countries in North Africa: Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia, Libya, Egypt, Sudan, Mauritania.
- Countries where Swahili is official or widely used: Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Democratic Republic of the Congo.
- Countries where French is an official language in West Africa: Senegal, Mali, Niger, Burkina Faso, Cote d'Ivoire, Benin, Togo, Guinea.

### Currency

Ask for countries by currency union or distinctive national currency.

- Eurozone countries: France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Ireland, Netherlands, Belgium, Austria, Greece, Finland, Slovakia, Slovenia, Croatia, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Cyprus.
- CFA franc users in West Africa: Senegal, Mali, Niger, Burkina Faso, Cote d'Ivoire, Benin, Togo, Guinea-Bissau.
- Dollarized countries: Ecuador, El Salvador, Panama.
- Countries using the pound sterling: United Kingdom.

### International Organizations

Ask for membership in major global or regional organizations.

- European Union members.
- NATO members.
- African Union members.
- ASEAN members: Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar, Brunei.
- Mercosur members: Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, Uruguay, Bolivia.

### Government And Political Geography

Ask for countries by government structure, capital arrangement, or sovereignty status.

- Countries with more than one capital: South Africa, Bolivia, Netherlands, Eswatini.
- Microstates in Europe: Andorra, Liechtenstein, Monaco, San Marino, Vatican.
- Constitutional monarchies: United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Japan, Thailand, Morocco, Jordan.

### Demographics

Ask for population or settlement-pattern facts that are not currently covered by capital prompts.

- Countries with populations over 100 million: China, India, United States, Indonesia, Pakistan, Nigeria, Brazil, Bangladesh, Russia, Mexico, Japan, Ethiopia, Philippines, Egypt, Vietnam.
- Countries with very high population density: Bangladesh, Singapore, Bahrain, Maldives, Malta.
- Countries with a primate city: France, Thailand, Argentina, Peru, Chile.
- Countries with a megacity that is not the capital: Brazil, Turkey, India, Pakistan, South Africa, United States.

### Economy And Exports

Ask for countries by globally recognizable industries or exports.

- Major oil exporters: Saudi Arabia, Iraq, Iran, Kuwait, United Arab Emirates, Qatar, Russia, Nigeria, Angola, Venezuela.
- Coffee producers: Brazil, Colombia, Ethiopia, Vietnam, Indonesia.
- Cocoa producers: Cote d'Ivoire, Ghana, Nigeria, Cameroon.
- Copper producers: Chile, Peru, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Zambia.
- Countries known for canal or shipping chokepoints: Egypt, Panama, Singapore, Turkey, Denmark.

### Time And Hemisphere

Ask for map-awareness facts that rely on global position without duplicating named-region prompts.

- Countries crossed by the equator: Ecuador, Colombia, Brazil, Gabon, Congo, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Uganda, Kenya, Somalia, Indonesia.
- Countries crossed by the Tropic of Cancer: Mexico, Egypt, Saudi Arabia, India, Bangladesh, China.
- Countries crossed by the Tropic of Capricorn: Chile, Argentina, Paraguay, Brazil, Namibia, Botswana, South Africa, Mozambique, Madagascar, Australia.
- Countries spanning many time zones: Russia, United States, Canada, Australia.

### Writing Systems And Scripts

Ask for countries associated with non-Latin writing systems.

- Countries using Cyrillic scripts: Russia, Belarus, Bulgaria, Serbia, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan.
- Countries using Arabic script: Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Iraq, Iran, Jordan, Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia.
- Countries using Devanagari: India, Nepal.
- Countries using Han characters in daily life: China, Japan, South Korea.

## Lower Priority Because They Overlap Existing Categories

- More capitals, flags, landmarks, borders, and coastal/region prompts. These are already heavily represented.
- More rivers, mountain ranges, deserts, and lakes. These are already covered under `Physical`.
- More ancient empires, former names, and colonial-era prompts. These are already covered under `History`.
- More animal habitat prompts. These are already present under `Wildlife`, though the category is still small.
- Enclave prompts. `vatican-enclave` and `border-lesotho-enclave` already cover this theme.
